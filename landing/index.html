Features

Built for developers who ship

Everything you need to monitor deploys, nothing you don't.

Turbo Polling

10-second refresh during active builds. Know the moment your deploy succeeds or fails.

Monorepo-Aware

Skipped builds show gray, not red. No more false-positive error alerts from filtered deploys.

Unified Dashboard

See Vercel and Netlify projects together. One glance shows everything that's building or recently deployed.

Smart Notifications

Desktop alerts when builds complete or fail. Stay focused until you need to know.

Secure Storage

API tokens stored in macOS Keychain. Your credentials never leave your machine.

Native SwiftUI

Built with SwiftUI for macOS. Lightweight, fast, and feels right at home.
